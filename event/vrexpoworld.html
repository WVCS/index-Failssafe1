How it works

For artists
- I -
Check out VR Expo World on the project's website and social media
- II -
Fill out the form with a description of the style/genre of your work, what exactly you plan to post (illustration/photo, animation/video, 3D model), etc.
- III -
After filling out the form, wait for the application to be processed. Usually it takes no more than 3 days
- IV -
After agreement, we place your work in the VR NFT Gallery and give the author permanent/temporary possession of the NFT, confirming the ownership of the space in the virtual art gallery. The author of the work can sell his NFT together with the artwork (or without it) through specialized marketplaces or use our services.
- V -
In addition to author works, we also provide an opportunity for collectors to post existing NFT works, original works of art, as well as your collections.
To apply
For companies (exhibitors)
- I -
Check out the material on the website, promos and features of VR Expo World
- II -
Fill out the brief with a description and characteristics of your product (for better interaction). A form to fill out is included.
- III -
Wait for the application to be processed. Usually it happens within 3 days.
- IV -
After confirmation and approval of the project, a separate exhibition space is created for your company with the addition of your products and integration into a single exhibition complex.

About company

A team of IT-specialists with specialized education and competencies. The main activities of our company are custom application development, video production and special effects. A full list of our services can be found in the "Services and Competencies" section.
